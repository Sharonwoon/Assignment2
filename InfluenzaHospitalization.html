Influenza Hospitalization Age Rates

The Influenza Hospitalization rate of All Ages, Children Aged 0-4 and Elderly Aged 65+

This chart presents the influenza hospitalization rates for different age groups, including children aged 0-4, all ages, and the elderly aged 65+, across various countries. The radial chart visualizes hospitalization rates for each country, with segments representing the different age categories. The chart uses distinct shades to differentiate between age groups, making it easier to compare the hospitalization rates for children, adults, and the elderly. Hovering over a segment displays detailed information about the specific age group, country, and its corresponding hospitalization rate. This chart helps highlight the global disparities in hospitalization rates across age groups for influenza.
