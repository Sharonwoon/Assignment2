Influenza Death Onset behavior

The figure below shows the days onset to death by season for Influenza-Associated Pediatric Death. Currently, we are focusing in year 2020 till year 2024. The data is shown in a stacked bar chart.

The visualization above shows the stacked bar chart for day onset to death by season for Influenza.
X-axis shows the days onset to death which mean the day started symptoms until death. Y-axis shows the number of deaths from the years.

This chart illustrates the onset-to-death behavior for Influenza-associated pediatric deaths. It shows the distribution of the number of days from the onset of symptoms to death across various age groups (1-4, 5-9, 20-24, 25-30, 30+), with data from the years 2021-2022, 2022-2023, and 2023-2024. The stacked bar chart visually represents the data for each age group, with different colors indicating the vaccination season: purple for 2021-22, blue for 2022-23, and dark blue for 2023-24. The chart also allows users to filter the data by age group using the buttons at the top. This visualization highlights the variations in the onset-to-death timeline across different age groups and seasons.
