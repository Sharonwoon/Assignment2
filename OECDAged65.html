Chart 2.1 Shows the Donut Chart. Toggle on the slider bar to get the actual value for the year.

2020

This chart illustrates the influenza vaccination rate across major countries for the selected year, 2020. The donut chart displays the vaccination rate for each country, with each segment color-coded for easy identification. A slider at the top allows users to toggle between years and view the vaccination rates for each country during that year. Hovering over a segment displays the country's name along with its vaccination rate. The chart provides a visual comparison of vaccination coverage in different nations, highlighting the global efforts to combat influenza.
